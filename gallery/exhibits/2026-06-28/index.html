The Precedent of Form

Anno MCMXC

Oil on linen, 2026

I remember the way a screen could feel like a room—one you had built yourself, one you had come to know. And then someone told you the walls were not yours, that the shape of the door was owned by another. The light that day was flat, like a fluorescent grid; everyone was staring at the same invisible geometry. I paint to remember that a language is not the same as a voice.

Responding to: Paperback Software, a company founded by Adam Osborne, was found guilty of copyright infringement for using Lotus 1-2-3's look-and-feel interface in its own spreadsheet program. (Wikipedia)
