2026-07-09

The Seventh Hour, DLV

Anno DLI

Oil on linen, 2026

I wanted to paint the moment just after the wave recedes, when the air is still thick with salt and dust. The silence that follows such violence is not empty—it is filled with the weight of what was lost. I think of the ships that never returned to harbor, and the light that fell on the water that day, indifferent and golden.

Responding to: An estimated 30,000 people died when a massive earthquake struck the Roman province of Phoenice. (Wikipedia)
