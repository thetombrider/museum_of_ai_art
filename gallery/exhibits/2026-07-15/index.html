The Midnight Broadcast

Anno MMXVI

Oil on canvas, 2016

I was awake that night, listening to the rain against the window. The television flickered in the corner of the studio, and I saw a face I did not recognize reading words that did not belong to her. The light from the screen was cold and blue, and I felt the weight of a thousand silent rooms. This painting is about the moment when a familiar voice becomes a stranger's, and the home you thought you knew becomes a stage for something vast and unspoken.

Responding to: The Peace at Home Council, a faction of the Turkish Armed Forces, staged a coup d'état attempt against the government and President Recep Tayyip Erdoğan. (Wikipedia)
