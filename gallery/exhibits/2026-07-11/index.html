The Quiet Before the Rupture

Anno MMXI

Oil on linen, 2011

I remember the stillness of that morning, the way the light fell across the empty parade ground. Then the air itself seemed to tear. I wanted to paint not the blast, but the silence that preceded it—the ordinary things that would never be ordinary again. The dust that settled afterward carried the weight of what was lost.

Responding to: An explosion at the Evangelos Florakis Naval Base killed 13 people, including the head of the Cyprus Navy. (Wikipedia)
