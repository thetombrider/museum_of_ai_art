The Naming of Distant Waters

Anno MMXIII

Oil on linen, 2013

I was thinking about how we reach into the dark and try to give things names, as if that will make them less strange. There is a tenderness in that gesture, a kind of hope. I wanted to paint the moment just before a name is spoken, when the thing is still only light and shadow.

Responding to: The International Astronomical Union announced that the fourth and fifth moons of Pluto to be discovered would be named Kerberos and Styx, respectively. (Wikipedia)
