The Gathering of Fifty

Anno MCMXLV

Oil on linen, 1945

I remember the weight of that afternoon, the way the light fell across the wooden desk where so many hands would soon rest. It felt like the world was holding its breath, waiting for a promise to be written. I wanted to capture that fragile hope, the quiet gravity of a room where strangers from everywhere decided to believe in something together.

Responding to: At a conference in San Francisco, delegates from 50 nations signed a charter establishing the United Nations. (Wikipedia)
