The Listener, MCMLXXIX

Anno MCMLXXIX

Oil on linen, 2025

I remember the first time I heard music that was mine alone. The world receded into a soft blur, leaving only the voice in my ear. This painting is an attempt to capture that moment of isolation and intimacy.

Responding to: Sony introduced the Walkman, a portable audio player that changed listening habits by offering users the ability to play one's own choice of music. (Wikipedia)
